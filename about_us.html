Get to Know Meal Mogul


Moto: “AT NO COST”


What we do:

Meal Mogul is a not for profit organization established in December 2020 that aims to distinguish food insecurity, promote physical and mental well being, and help contribute towards efforts to end hunger, starting at home, here in Canada. We serve anyone looking for their next meal, ranging from 1 to upto 5 meals a day.


Why?

Time and time again, research has proven that public interventions at the federal level that aim to modestly increase income levels of Canadians, make a notable impact in addressing food insecurity in Canada. And while we may not be at that point right now, we ALL know charity begins at home! Checkout our work in progress or what we have planned for the future. As always, we will continue to do our part in making as many resources and supports publicly available to anyone at the tip of their fingers, and that too, AT NO COST.


Mission statement:

Our criteria is simple: No matter who you are , what your background is, or your current situation, we here at Meal Mogul ensure NO ONE goes hungry! We do the hard work, we connect the dots! We’ll provide the resources + support you need, so you don’t have to think where your next meal will come from.
