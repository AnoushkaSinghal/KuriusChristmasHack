Who are we?

At Meal Mogul, we make the process simple. If you are ever in need of food, we are here to help. Use our easy-to-use CANADA WIDE map to locate ANY food banks near you.
Get the help you need,NOW!

Meal Mogul has 3 goals that define our organization:

Firstly, we encourage and try our best to widely share information about food banks on a publicly available platform.

Secondly, as agents of change, we aspire to see REAL PROGRESS. Hency why we are partnering up with organizations such as the Food Banks Canada, Community Food Centers, Food Secure Canada and Dieticians of Canada among others, to collaborate on policy decisions in the future that address the root of food insecurity.

Thirdly, even though we believe NO ONE has ever become poor from giving, we GIVE, and we keep on GIVING. BUT, we ALways encourage anyone who has benefitted from our programs to give back to the community! Whether that's through sharing your time with us as a volunteer, helping us feed others,or by using your skills! All benefactors and non benefactors are encouraged to participate! E.g: If you know a thing or two about fixing cars, hold a mechanics workshop for your local community through us! Or tutor the kids that have found a perfect shelter at one of our partner organizations! Whatever it may be, it's DOABLE here!
